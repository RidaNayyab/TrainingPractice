## 🎬 What Happens When Teacher Opens Training

```
1. FEEDBACK STEP (Automatic)
   ↓ Shows transcription of their lesson
   ↓ Shows AI-generated feedback
   ↓ Lists improvement areas
   ↓ Teacher clicks "View Training Resources"

2. TRAINING STEP
   ↓ Shows training video
   ↓ Lists recommended resources
   ↓ Teacher reads/watches content
   ↓ Teacher clicks "Start Practice Questions"

3. PRACTICE STEP
   ↓ Shows 1-2 scenario questions
   ↓ Teacher types responses
   ↓ Teacher submits practice
   ↓ System records completion

4. COMPLETION STEP
   ↓ Shows success message
   ↓ Summary of learning
   ↓ Teacher clicks "Done"
   ↓ Returns to niete app dashboard
```

## 🚨 If You Encounter Issues

**API won't start?**
- Check `.env.local` has DB credentials
- Verify internet connection
- Check port 3001 isn't in use

**Component not loading?**
- Verify API is running on port 3001
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure teacherId exists in database

**Need to customize?**
- See `INTEGRATION_GUIDE.md` for customization tips
- Update training resources in `src/server.ts`
- Modify feedback display in `FeedbackTrainingModule.tsx`
